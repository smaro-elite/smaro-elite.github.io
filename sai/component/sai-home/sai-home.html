SAI: SingleAccessInterface

Badge  

For better understanding, lets have a real life scenario analysis :

Scenario
  • You own a personal laptop
  • Definitely you would be login into some popular web application like messenger, gmail, etc regularly
  • No one love to sign in everytime, So most of the time we select the remember me option on such web application
  • Suppose anyone (let say your friend) ask you for your personal laptop
  • Now the question arises, Can you give them your personal laptop instantly ?
  • Obvious Answer is No ! Why so ? Because I believe you dont want to share your personal data from your logged in web application or you dont want to reveal your browsing history
Solution
Lets see, how you can share your personal laptop without leaking any personal data
  • Go to each website, log out one by one and hand it over to them
  • Go to browser setting, clear out your history and cache
Is this the only way ? No
  • You can choose to stay close to your laptop and stick your eyes on screen as long as they are using your laptop
These solution`s makes you frustated ? If Yes
  • Then the solution to your issue is SingleAccessInterface a.k.a. SAI
Thinking How ? Me too
  • SAI has in-built centralised single password login system.
  • Just sing in once and all your application is made available to you
  • Close it, It will lock down automatically
  • Someone tried to enter forcefully, it can detect it and destroy your personal data that are saved locally
